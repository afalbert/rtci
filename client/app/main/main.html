Regional Transit Capital Inventory

Welcome to the RTCI Website and Data Portal. This page is hosted by the Metropolitan Transportation Commission for the transit operators located in the San Francisco Bay Area Region. Access to this website is restricted to MTC staff and users authorized by the primary RTCI contact at each partner agency.

The RTCI is a comprehensive regional database of the transit assets that are owned by transit agencies in the San Francisco Bay Area. The information contained in the database is provided by Bay Area operators and represents a significant effort to ensure that assets in the region are maintained in a state of good repair.

Our website has been recently updated!

This new version of the RTCI website was developed by MTC staff to simplify the data collection and submission process. Operators will now be able to:

  • Edit their asset records directly online instead of having to export/import, update and export again.
  • Download their entire dataset and/or specified tables as an excel file by clicking a button on the webpage.
  • Visualize data and current state of good repair with the dashboard functionality.
